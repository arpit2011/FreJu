About Us

We are a group of grad students bringing to you, our love for fresh juices.

Fresh juice, though a sought after beverage are hard to find in the US. Making juice on your own is a task. Grocery store runs, cleaning, peeling and chopping the fruits and vegetables, running the mixer for juice and the infamous cleanup after often makes you say NO to the Nectar altogether.

BUT fret not, we’ve got your back. We will deliver fresh-pressed juice to your doorstep.
